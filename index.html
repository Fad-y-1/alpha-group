Alfa Group

Alfa Group is a leading industrial cleaning solutions company specialized in supplying premium cleaning products for hotels, hospitals, restaurants, resorts, and large commercial facilities.

Since 2008, Alfa Group has focused on combining powerful cleaning performance, modern technology, and elegant presentation to help large businesses maintain the highest standards of hygiene and comfort.







Why Businesses Trust Alfa Group

Alfa Group was founded in Hurghada in 2008 as a small local supplier specialized in industrial cleaning solutions for hotels and tourist facilities across the Red Sea region.

The company started with a simple vision: providing high-quality cleaning products capable of handling the demanding daily operations inside hotels, resorts, restaurants, and healthcare facilities.

During its early years, Alfa Group worked with several local restaurants and boutique hotels in Hurghada. Thanks to the quality of its products and reliable service, the company quickly gained the trust of larger hospitality brands and medical facilities.

By 2012, Alfa Group expanded its operations and began supplying industrial detergents, disinfectants, kitchen degreasers, and sanitation systems to major hotels, tourist villages, and hospitals across Hurghada and the Red Sea coast.

Over the years, the company became known for combining powerful performance with elegant presentation and long-lasting freshness, helping facilities maintain premium hygiene standards while improving the experience of guests and visitors.

Today, Alfa Group proudly serves a wide range of clients including:

• Luxury Hotels & Resorts
• Restaurants & Industrial Kitchens
• Hospitals & Healthcare Facilities
• Tourist Villages & Entertainment Facilities
• Commercial Buildings & Large Establishments

The company continues to grow by focusing on innovation, quality, customer trust, and delivering industrial cleaning solutions tailored for the hospitality and healthcare industries.
